Rico · Nikko Blue Stupid Kid

This is the second master I have done for this group roughly a month later. As like before, my task was to master there work. I brought out more of the low and middle frequencies while slightly lowering the high frequencies. Then I compressed the track a little to make it sound a bit more balanced. I expanded the sound to make it sound fuller and more engaging. Finally, I raised the gain by 7 dB and added a limiter so that it will not peak.

Rico · Mastered Track Artist Robert Crawford

This is my first professional work as a Freelance Sound Designer. I work with an upcoming Artist by the name of Robert Crawford. He sent me his file on one of his songs and my job was to master his track. The only requirement was to not alter his voice while enhancing the elements of his song to bring it up a nocte. I was able make the track sound fuller and more whole while keeping his vocals the same with a bit more clarity. The piece was made by him, I was able to emphasize and clean up on his work.
