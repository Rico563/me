Rico · Final Tribe

ReggieCon, Final draft As the title may suggest, this is my final draft for my attempt to create an intro for ReggieCon. In my final draft, I decided to go with my first draft, Tribe, as it seemed to be a better quality intro, in my opinion. I kept it mostly the same with improvements on the technical side of things to make it sound less robotic and more natural. I done this with the best of my abilities and by no means is this perfect. Still, I enjoyed what I had created and I hope you all enjoy as well.

Rico · Saturday Night

This file was an attempt for me to immerse the listener into the surrounding and listen to the actions and events of this character. My character decided to drive in the rain to spend his evening at a famous 4 star restaurant. After a delicious meal he drives back home to have his night ruined by a police pulling him over. Lucky nothing serious comes from it, only the typically annoying ticket. After arriving to his home from the amazing food and surprising scare, he jumps into bed and instantaneously falls to sleep. Hope everyone enjoys.
